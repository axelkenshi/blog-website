## üõ°Ô∏è Ethics and Security in Data Management  

The power of data comes with great responsibility. Without ethics and security, data can shift from asset to threat.  

- **Individual privacy:** Personal data must be protected from misuse, manipulation, or exploitation.  
- **Transparency:** Companies and institutions should explain how data is collected and used.  
- **System security:** Safeguards against leaks, hacks, and misuse are essential.  
- **Ethical use:** Data should be applied to improve quality of life, not to control or harm.  
- **Fair access:** Data should not be monopolized by a few; knowledge distribution must remain equitable.  

> *‚ÄúOwning data is power, but using it ethically is wisdom.‚Äù*
