## üõ†Ô∏è Practical Exercises to Strengthen Mental Calculation  

1. **Daily multiplication drills:** Spend 5 minutes revisiting multiplication tables to rebuild speed and confidence.  
2. **Estimation practice:** When shopping, estimate totals before reaching the cashier. Compare with the actual bill.  
3. **Mental percentage games:** Calculate discounts (10%, 25%, 50%) mentally when browsing prices.  
4. **Number puzzles:** Engage with Sudoku, math riddles, or mental arithmetic apps to make practice enjoyable.  
5. **Time and distance calculations:** Estimate travel times or distances without GPS, then check accuracy.  
6. **Budget challenges:** Track expenses mentally throughout the day, then verify against receipts.
